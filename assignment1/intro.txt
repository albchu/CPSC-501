ESCAPE FROM THE WOODS ------------------------ Your party 'P' is lost in the middle of the greenwood and you must guide them safely to exit 'E'. It's not as easy as it sounds though, the horrors of wood decreases your party's courage over time. The longer you take to escape the weaker you will get. If your courage gets too low then your party may panic and run off randomly into the woods. If your courage reaches zero then your party has died of fright and you lose the game!  Further challenges abound, scary magic tokens 'x' randomly appear. If your party gets close enough to them then their courage will quickly drain away.  Are you up to the challenge of saving your hapless party? Find out now and see if you can ESCAPE FROM THE WOODS!!!
